Unleash Your Inner Dog Lover

Dogs, our faithful companions, have evolved alongside us for millennia. From their wild ancestors to the diverse breeds we cherish today, their genetic journey reflects adaptation, selective breeding, and harmonious coexistence with humans.

From Wolves to Breeds: The gray wolf (Canis lupus) serves as the common ancestor of all dogs. Over time, humans selectively bred wolves, shaping them into the remarkable array of breeds we know today. From tiny Chihuahuas to majestic Great Danes, each breed tells a captivating story of evolution.
